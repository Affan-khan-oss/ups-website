Frequently Asked Questions

What is the difference between Online and Offline UPS?

An Online UPS provides continuous power with zero transfer time and better protection, while an Offline UPS switches to battery power only when utility power fails, making it more affordable but with a brief transfer time.

How long will my UPS provide backup power?

Backup time depends on the UPS capacity and the load connected. Most UPS systems provide 5-15 minutes of runtime at full load, which is typically enough to safely shut down equipment or switch to a generator.

What size UPS do I need?

To determine the right UPS size, calculate the total power consumption (in watts) of all equipment you need to protect, then add 20-30% for future expansion. Our sales team can help you with this calculation.

How often should I replace my UPS batteries?

Most UPS batteries last 3-5 years, depending on usage conditions and temperature. We recommend replacing batteries every 3 years for critical applications or when the battery test indicates reduced capacity.
